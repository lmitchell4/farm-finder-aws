Finder

Farm Finder is a place for food-growers to come together to share their harvests, growing tips, and support. We have a loose definition of farm - so whether yours is a kitchen planter, a backyard garden, a community garden, or a full-blown operation with tractors - you are welcome. You're also welcome if you don't have a farm but are on the hunt for something home-grown. As we like to say around here: If you eat, you are in!

Browse the farms of community members in your area. When you're ready to barter or buy something from a fellow farmer, simply sign in and contact them.
